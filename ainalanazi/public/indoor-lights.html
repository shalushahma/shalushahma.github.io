COB Down Light Series

This series includes recessed focus lights (single point of the light source), directional lights, and reflectors to highlight various items. Due to the perfect combination of fit and finish with exemplary performance, this series is the preferred choice of architects. These are best applicable in showrooms for product display, homes as accent lighting for highlighting showpieces, hotels, restaurants, and create contrast lighting to set the right mood.

PRODUCT FEATURES:
  • Perfect replacement of the traditional lights
  • Saves 80% electricity cost
  • Better performance
  • Easy installation

LED Track Light Series

In these lighting series, light fixtures are attached anywhere on a continuous track rail which is electrically charged. So, there is no need for wiring to individual light positions. It poses numerous advantages such as energy efficiency, longer lifespan, lesser heat emission, relatively inexpensive and versatility to add or remove. This compact size track spotlight is designed to be used as an accent light or task light and mostly can be seen in commercial places like retail stores, shopping malls, showrooms, and offices because of its directional lighting.

PRODUCT FEATURES:
  • Optical anti-glare design
  • Low maintenance
  • Low power consumption
  • Specially designed for indoor use
